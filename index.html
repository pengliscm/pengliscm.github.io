Academic Background

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Computer Science and Mathematics Division at Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL). I received my Ph.D. in the Department of Mathematics from Iowa State University in 2019 under the supervision of Prof. Hailiang Liu and Prof. Songting Luo. Prior to joining ORNL, I worked as a Post-Doc Fellow in the Department of Mathematics at Wayne State University, a Research Assistant Professor (research-track) in the Department of Radiation Oncology at the University of Kansas.

I work in the area of Computational Mathematics and Applied Mathematics with the main focus on Numerical Analysis, Partial Differential Equations, and Scientific Computing. My work reflects a strong interplay of rigorous mathematical analysis, the design and implementation of accurate and efficient numerical algorithms for partial differential equations, and their applications to physics, astrophysics, engineering, biology, energy, and medicine.

My recent research focuses on
   • IPDG method, (Positivity-preserving) DDG method, Penalty free DG method;
   • IEQ, SAV, RK, BDF1-3;
   • Numerical methods for gradient flows including Allen-Cahn equation, Cahn-Hilliard equation, and Swift-Hohenberg equation, etc;
   • FEM/Adaptive FEM for ellptic/Stokes equation with singularity, and $C^0$ FEM for biharmonic/triharmonic equation on a nonconvex polygon;
   * Deep learning; Kinetic equation; Transport equation with applications in medical problem, and Neutrino-Matter, etc.

I am also an active referee for many journals, such as Journal of Scientific Computing, Journal of Computational and Applied Mathematics, Numerical Algorithms, etc.

Research Interests

• Numerical methods for PDEs with high order spatial derivatives - FEM, DG, DDG, IPDG, Mixed DG, penalty free DG, etc.

• Numerical methods for gradient flows - BDF1-3, CN, convex splitting, IEQ, SAV, RK, etc.

• Numerical methods for conservation laws, Hamilton–Jacobi equations.

• Numerical methods for transport equations - Application in medical problems, astrophysics, etc.

• Numerical methods for kinetic equations - Asymptotic method, etc.

• Theories and numerical methods for PDEs with singularities - elliptic, biharmonic, sixth order (triharmonic), Stokes equations, etc.

• Maximum principle preserving numerical methods.

• Computational high frequency wave propagation.

• Mathematical theory of deep learning.
